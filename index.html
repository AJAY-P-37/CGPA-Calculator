CGPA/GPA Calculator

  • All the fields are mandatory to enable the "Calculate" Button

  • In CGPA Section,

    • If you have/had any backlogs, you must re-enter the correct number for "Total Credits Enrolled" field, or else just leave it to be updated automatically

    • "Total Credits Enrolled" is the Sum of all subject's credits enrolled only Once and ignore even if you have/had a backlog and enrolled again in another semester

    • Example: (upto semester 2)

      • semester 1: 7.00 GPA x 20 Enrolled credits (1 arrear in 3 credits subject)

      • semester 2: 8.00 GPA x 23 Enrolled credits (including previous 1 arrear, so only 20 credits in this semester)

      • Total Credits Enrolled: 40 [20(credits in sem1 subjects) + 20(credits in sem2 subjects)]. '43' will be updated automatically, but "change it to 40".

      • ((7.00 x 20) + (8.00 x 23)) / (20 + 20) = 8.10 is the CGPA in semester 2

  • In GPA Section,

    • Incase of any backlogs, select "U grade" and enter it's respective credits
